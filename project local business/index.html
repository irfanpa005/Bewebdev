About Us

Associated Medicals, nestled in the heart of Calicut city, has been a beloved establishment for pet lovers since its inception in 2008. With over a decade of dedicated service, we have become a trusted name in the local pet care industry. At Associated Medicals, we understand the unique bond between pets and their owners, and our mission has always been to ensure the health and happiness of your furry companions. We offer a wide range of services, from high-quality pet food and supplies to expert advice and compassionate care. Our comprehensive pet care includes providing all necessary medicines and vaccines for all pets, ensuring their well-being throughout their lives. We take pride in being a part of the Calicut community, and we look forward to continuing our journey of providing top-notch pet care for many more years to come.
